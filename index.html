About RSForge

RSForge is a locally owned IT support company proudly serving the Kentuckiana region. Founded by residents Ethan Rowe and Jacob Scott, our mission is simple: to provide small businesses with the kind of fast, honest, and personal tech support they deserve — without inflated prices, long wait times, or cookie-cutter service. We started RSForge because we saw too many small businesses struggling with unreliable IT help, confusing technical jargon, or large service providers that didn’t care. That’s why we’re committed to doing things differently. We bring a community-first mindset to every job, treating your business like it’s our own. Whether you need someone to fix a frustrating computer issue, set up a secure network, migrate your data, or manage your entire IT infrastructure — we’re here to help. No red tape. No upselling. Just dependable, down-to-earth support from people who know your community because we’re part of it. At RSForge, we believe in building long-term relationships, not one-time fixes. When you work with us, you get more than an IT provider — you get a trusted partner who cares about your success.
